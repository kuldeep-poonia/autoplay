AutoApply Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection and Storage

AutoApply operates on a strict Local-First Architecture. All data you enter (including your profile information, work experience, resumes, API keys, and application history) is stored 100% locally on your device using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API.

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on any external servers. Your data never leaves your browser.

2. Third-Party Services (Google Gemini API)

If you choose to use the AI Answer Generation feature, your specific question context and a subset of your profile data are sent securely and directly to the Google Gemini API using the API key you provide. AutoApply acts only as a passthrough. We do not proxy, intercept, or log these requests.

3. Usage Tracking

We do not track your browsing history. The extension passively monitors URLs locally to detect job application sites solely for the purpose of indicating readiness. It does not send this URL data anywhere.

4. Changes to this Policy

If we update our privacy practices, we will update this document within the extension. Since we collect no data, we have no means to notify you via email.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
